Terms of Use

It is the goal of Plink to maintain a safe environment for all users. We acknowledge that in order to accomplish this we must layout rules and emphasis that any who violate these rules while using the service may be suspended or banned

Definitions

Content – means any input by the user
Post - means the data sent between the client and server which make up the visible square ‘cards’ containing information on the posting user and what they had entered to be shared

Rules

I. Plink will not tolerate any form of abusive, offensive or explicit content by any user
II. Any post uploaded to Plink is assumed to be the users own work or appropriately accredited to the original creator
III. Admins have full right to deny access to Plink to anyone including those already using the service
IV. Admins have full right to the destruction, storage or manipulation of any content uploaded to Plink
V. While posts uploaded to Plink are conventionally only shown to registered users it should be noted that they could be viewed by anyone outside of registered users and students
VI. Any user under a username or first name which is inappropriate or subject to impersonation may be changed without notice
