Launching Soon

Welcome to Plink. A new entertainment platform created exclusively for students at selected schools across New Zealand. Plink is based on the idea of sharing content for the entertainment of others. Take what you like and share it through Plink to be shown to others with similar interests and build connections across the network.

Privacy

Plink does not require any personal information. We keep your identity secret to you and your friends by requiring only a first name and username for your profile. To keep an authentic user base of students Plink will also ask for school emails to sift out fake profiles or registrations from non-students.

No old Posts

Plinks maintains an ephemeral nature by removing posts after 2 weeks. We understand that opinions and interests change so we designed Plink to reflect that. Be comfortable in the knowledge that your posts today won’t embarrass or contradict your opinions later on.

Levels

Plink has taken the step to introduce levels into social media. Gather influence points from every subscriber, admirer, endorsement and repost you earn to rise through the ranks. The higher your level the wider an audience you reach with every post. Get in early to secure as much influence as you can to become your own internet celebrity.

Web Application Design

Plink is based on a single page hash-based navigation system. In other words Plink’s design makes page switching much faster and requires much less internet usage. This design also allows you to view user profiles without affecting your position or progress in the main post feed.

New incentives for Reposting

Finally, users will be more inclined to share your content by getting rewarded. Whenever a user reposts your post any endorsements made through it will give both the reposter and original poster influence points.

Doesn’t Share Unnecessary Information

Plink keeps your interests and connections private. This includes what you endorse or comment on. Plink also keeps your subscriptions private from the prying eye of the public. Plink only cares about sharing what YOU want to be shared while keeping the rest hidden for your discretion.
